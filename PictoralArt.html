Reflection

Medium:Acrylic

Date: 9/27/2014

For this painting I wanted to work with the idea of symmetry and balance. If I were to fold the piece in half I wanted everything to be the same. I thought of different things in the natural world that were symmetrical. The first one that came to mind was a butterfly. I drew a face within each of its wings. Then I noticed that there was two more faces that appeared one on each edge of the paper that looked ghostlike. Peering closer and paying attention to detail I noticed two more faced one on each top portion of the wing looking like it was screaming with its mouth open.
