Artist Statement

Without the ability to actualize an idea or concept, art would not exist. My art is continiously looking for the best way to interpret my ideas. I am open to using many different mediums and styles to express myself in the best way possible. Each piece I create is meaningful in its own way. I would like my work to evoke something in the viewer making them ponder as to what is presented in front of them. What does it mean? That would make my work sucessful.
